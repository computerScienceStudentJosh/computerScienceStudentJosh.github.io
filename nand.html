NAND GATES

NAND Gates take two inputs and are the mirror to the AND gates as NOR gates are to OR gates
INPUTS OUTPUTS
0\OFF 0\OFF 1\ON
1\ON 0\OFF 1\ON
0\OFF 1\ON 1\ON
1\ON 1\ON 0\OFF
they also work similarly to pressing shift to capitalise a letter with caps lock on
