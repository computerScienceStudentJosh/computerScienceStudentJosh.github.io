AND GATES

And Gates take two inputs and if those inputs are both ON then the output will be ON too
as shown below.

INPUTS OUTPUTS
0\OFF 0\OFF 0\OFF
1\ON 0\OFF 0\OFF
0\OFF 1\ON 0\OFF
1\ON 1\ON 1\ON

AND gates are used when there must be a specific input for a given output
such as needing to hold shift and a letter to capitalise it
