NOR GATES

NOR Gates take two inputs and act similarly to OR gates but inverted as it is made by attaching a NOT gate to an OR gate
as such its outputs are the opposite to the OR gates
INPUTS OUTPUTS
0\OFF 0\OFF 1\ON
1\ON 0\OFF 0\OFF
0\OFF 1\ON 0\OFF
1\ON 1\ON 0\OFF
they also work similarly to pressing shift to capitalise a letter with caps lock on
